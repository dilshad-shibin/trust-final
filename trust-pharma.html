Trust Pharma

Trust Pharma is our Allopathic manufacturing division was started on 3rd September 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency. This division was created on the sole focus on manufacturing hand sanitizers and other COVID-19 safety products. This firm was named after our first business venture ‘Trust pharma’ which was started in 1994 (vaccine and specialty distribution) which was later discontinued in 1998 after successful four years business.
