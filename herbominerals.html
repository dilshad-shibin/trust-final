Our Story

TRUST Herbominerals started on 26th January 2006 is our Homeopathic Manufacturing unit located in Manjummel, Ernakulam district. With 14 years of experience and over 100 employees currently we have operations all over Kerala with representatives covering every district. Few of our main products include tinctures, mother tinctures, dilutions, ointments, oils, sugar globules, blank tablets, milk of sugar and few of our very own patented products. Due to the Covid pandemic emergency we started our own homeopathic sanitizer under the brand name Trust Herbominerals.
